Delicious Chicken Curry

Chicken Curry is a flavorful and aromatic dish popular in South Asian cuisine. This recipe is easy to prepare and perfect for family dinners. It pairs well with rice or naan, making it a comforting and satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2 onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1-inch ginger (grated)
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add minced garlic and grated ginger; cook for a minute.
  4. Mix in the pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil separates.
  5. Add turmeric, cumin, coriander, red chili powder, and salt; stir well.
  6. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides.
  7. Pour in water, cover, and let it simmer for 20 minutes.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala and mix well.
  9. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan.
